About Us

The Scientific Society, started somewhere in the year 2018 by two (then) young souls, Lajja Patel and Aditya Agarwal under the aegis of Varad Puntambekar and Vasishta Polisetty, has evolved in its vision manifolds from its modest origins.
Originally a platform for science nerds to rant, for tips and experiences to be shared effectively, the Scientific Society has now begun to host large stake events at high school, undergraduate levels and beyond. Its primary aim has evolved to not only encourage undergraduate research at AIIMS, but also facilitate programs, internships and grants.
